About The Event

PRAESTO 2K24 is a three-day event designed for students, tech enthusiasts, and professionals. It offers a platform to explore the latest tech trends, network with industry peers, and enjoy learning and entertainment. The event features workshops, interactive sessions with experts, project creation opportunities, and a showcase of projects with prizes.

F.A.Q

  • Can you provide details about the guest speakers or experts who will be attending?

    Guest speakers and experts attending Praesto this year include professionals from leading tech companies, academic researchers, and entrepreneurs with expertise in various fields such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and renewable energy.

  • Is there any specific theme for this year's Praesto?

    The specific theme for this year's Praesto is "Innovate, Collaborate, Transform," focusing on encouraging innovation, fostering collaboration, and driving transformation in the tech industry.

  • Are there any opportunities for networking or collaboration with industry professionals?

    Praesto provides opportunities for networking and collaboration with industry professionals through panel discussions, networking sessions, and career fairs where students can interact with recruiters and experts from various companies.

  • Can you provide examples of real-world web development projects?

    It's a project based workshop where u will be taught how to build a project by the end of day 2
